Key Benefits for Students

  • Easy Application: Our intuitive online portal makes applying for bursaries quick and straightforward.
  • Real-time Tracking: Track the status of your application from submission to disbursement.
  • Secure Data: Your personal and financial information is protected by industry-leading security measures.
  • Centralized Information: Access all bursary opportunities in one place.

Key Benefits for Administrators

  • Streamlined Workflow: Automate the bursary management process.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Generate comprehensive reports and analytics.
  • Improved Efficiency: Reduce administrative overhead.
  • Enhanced Transparency: Maintain a clear and auditable record.

Bursary Criteria

  • Academic Merit: Maintain a minimum GPA and strong academic record.
  • Financial Need: Provide documentation demonstrating financial hardship.
  • Field of Study: Some bursaries are targeted at specific fields like STEM or Education.
  • Community Involvement: Participation in extracurricular activities may be considered.
